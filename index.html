Petroleum Geologist

    Summary

    My goal as a professional is to apply and expand my knowledge in the development of conventional and unconventional oil reservoirs. I have worked as a consulting geologist in Mexico for two years, integrating geologic information for static model improvement and estimating hydrocarbon reserves.
    Additionally, I have participated in planning complex horizontal wells using Landmark or Petrel software tools. As an operations geologist in Venezuela, I have had the oportunity to work on sites of well operations, geosteering complex horizontal wells in thin pay sands of the Orinoco Heavy Oil Belt.
    I am also Fluent in English and Spanish, which has led me to job opportunities teaching professionals in the oil business so that they could increase their skills in the industry.
